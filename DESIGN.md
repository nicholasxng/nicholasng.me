# Amber Tech Narrative

A professional portfolio design system for Nicholas Ng, emphasizing high-impact delivery, technical excellence, and strategic execution.

## Visual Principles
- **Minimalist & High-Contrast**: Uses a clean light surface with sharp black text and bold amber accents.
- **Narrative-Driven**: Typography is scaled for readability and impact, supporting deep-dive professional stories.
- **Modern Tech Aesthetic**: Utilizes the Epilogue typeface for a contemporary, structured feel.

## Key Components
- **Top Navigation**: Fixed, high-contrast bar with uppercase labels and a clear "Download CV" primary action.
- **Interactive Sidebar**: Used in the Experience section to navigate between roles, utilizing amber for active states.
- **Bento-style Grid**: Organized layouts for experience summaries and skills.
